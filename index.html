About Craffty Creatures

Small studio, big imagination

Craffty Creatures is a home-based craft studio creating made-to-order designs. We focus on personalised pieces that feel warm, playful and memorable.

What makes us different

Each item is made after your order is confirmed – colours, names and themes are customised for you.

  • Hand-painted details and finishing
  • Packed carefully for gifting

  • Teacher gifts, friendship gifts and more
